Website Description

Songtuary is a free and open source music streaming service. It is an alternative to other music streaming services such as Spotify, Apple Music, and Youtube Music. Songtuary is currently in beta. Some features are still being developed. Songtuary is available on the web and on mobile. We offer a range of services with corresponding pricing options to meet your needs.
