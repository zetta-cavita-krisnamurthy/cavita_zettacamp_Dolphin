Who are we?

We are a group of second year students from the University of the Yogyakarta - Amikom. We are currently taking up BS Computer Science.

Our goal is to provide a free and open source music streaming service.

We believe that music is a universal language that everyone can understand. We also believe that music should be accessible to everyone.

What is Songtuary?

Songtuary is a free and open source music streaming service. It is an alternative to other music streaming services such as Spotify, Apple Music, and Youtube Music.

Songtuary is currently in beta. Some features are still being developed.

Songtuary is available on the web and on mobile.

Our Services

The service offers two options: "Free" and "Personal." The "Free" option provides 480p video quality and 128kbps audio quality, accompanied by ads during usage. Offline viewing is not available, but users can utilize the shuffle feature. On the other hand, the "Personal" option requires registration and offers 720p video quality, 320kbps audio quality, and an ad-free experience. It allows offline viewing and also includes the shuffle feature.
